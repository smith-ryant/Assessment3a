About Me

Salutations! Allow me to introduce myself. I am Ryan Smith, currently fulfilling the role of a Product Manager for a medical device company. But that's not all I aspire to be. Presently, I am exploring the field of software engineering with a learning endeavor at DevMountain.

I live in the scenic locale of Twin Falls, ID. Here, I am blessed with a lively and love-filled family, comprising my spouse, our lovely daughter, and our four quirky pets; two cats and two dogs. The result? There is never a dull moment in our household.

When it comes to personal interests, music holds a special place in my heart. Not only do I appreciate its soothing allure, but I am also an avid collector, trader, and seller of guitars, nurturing a small but cherished collection. To celebrate this passion, I am on a delightful journey, learning the chords of guitar and the tunes of piano.

As for my career vision, it is a blend of professional growth and personal fulfillment. Envisaging a kind of progressive retirement, I aim to create software solutions for the company I am associated with while simultaneously building a client base in the capacity of a freelance Software Engineer.
